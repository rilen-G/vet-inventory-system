# Product Brief

## Project name
Web-Based Inventory, Sales Invoice, and Payment Monitoring System for Veterinary Product Distribution

## Client profile
The client is a veterinary products distribution business.

## Problem
The client currently relies on spreadsheet-based inventory tracking and manual handwritten receipts.
This creates inefficiency, limited visibility, and risk of stock/invoice inconsistencies.

## Proposed solution
A web-based system that centralizes:
- inventory tracking
- sales invoices
- receipts and payments
- stock updates
- low stock and expiry alerts

## Why web-based
The client did not explicitly require installed desktop software.
They want the system available on 2 computers, and phone access is acceptable.
A web app is the most practical prototype and delivery format.

## Intended users
- office staff
- at most 2 concurrent users
- shared access, no separate accounts needed for prototype

## Main value of the system
- reduces manual work
- keeps stock updated automatically
- improves invoice and payment tracking
- makes reports and printable documents easier
- keeps data centralized across devices

## Core prototype promise
This is a working prototype designed around the client’s workflow.
It is meant to demonstrate how the final system would operate and can be refined based on client feedback.

## Success criteria
The client should be able to understand:
1. how inventory is stored and monitored
2. how invoices are created and finalized
3. how stock is deducted and restored
4. how payments are recorded
5. how receipts and reports are generated
